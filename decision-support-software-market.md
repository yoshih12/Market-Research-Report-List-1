<p><p>The decision support software market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based decision support software refers to the applications that are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It allows users to access and use the software from anywhere with an internet connection, offering flexibility and scalability. On-premise decision support software, on the other hand, is installed and hosted on the user's own servers or computers, providing full control and security but limited accessibility outside the organization's premises.</p></p>
