<p><p>The Pneumatic Vises market offers two main types of vises: Horizontal Vises and Vertical Vises. Horizontal Vises are designed to hold and clamp workpieces horizontally, with jaws that move in a horizontal direction to secure the material. They are suitable for tasks such as milling, drilling, and light machining. On the other hand, Vertical Vises are used to securely hold and clamp workpieces in a vertical position. These vises are commonly employed in applications like grinding, shaping, and cutting operations.</p></p>
