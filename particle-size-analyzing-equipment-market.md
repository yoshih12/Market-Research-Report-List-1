<p><strong>The Particle Size Analyzing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Diffraction</li><li>Dynamic Light Scattering</li><li>Imaging Analysis</li><li>Coulter Principle</li><li>Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Particle Size Analyzing Equipment market consists of various types of instruments used to determine the size of particles present in a sample. Laser Diffraction works by measuring the diffraction pattern caused by particles passing through a laser beam. Dynamic Light Scattering analyzes the fluctuations in the scattered light to determine particle size. Imaging Analysis captures images of particles and calculates their size. Coulter Principle measures changes in electrical conductivity when particles pass through a small orifice. Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis tracks the movement of particles under Brownian motion. Others include various methods such as sieving and sedimentation for particle size analysis.</p></p>
