<p><ul><li>Single Junction</li><li>Multi-junction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous Silicon Solar Cells are a type of thin-film solar technology that is flexible and lightweight. The market for these cells can be divided into two types: Single Junction and Multi-junction. Single Junction amorphous silicon solar cells have a single layer of amorphous silicon, making them less expensive but also less efficient. On the other hand, Multi-junction amorphous silicon solar cells consist of multiple layers of amorphous silicon, which increases their efficiency but also their cost. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications depending on the desired level of efficiency and cost.</p></p>
