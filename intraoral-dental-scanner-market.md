<p><p>An intraoral dental scanner is a device used by dentists to capture accurate 3D images of a patient's teeth and gums. These scanners are a modern and efficient alternative to traditional dental impression methods, such as using dental putty or trays, as they provide a more comfortable and convenient experience for both the patient and the dentist. Intraoral dental scanners use various technologies, such as optical and laser scanning, to capture detailed images that can be used for various dental applications, including restorative dentistry, orthodontics, and dental prosthetics.</p><p>The market for intraoral dental scanners has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing adoption of digital dentistry, along with advancements in technology and the growing demand for minimally invasive and non-invasive dental procedures, are the primary drivers of market growth. Furthermore, the accuracy, efficiency, and convenience offered by intraoral dental scanners have made them increasingly popular among dental professionals and patients alike.</p><p>Additionally, the rise in dental tourism, the growing geriatric population, and the increasing prevalence of dental disorders are further fueling market growth. Moreover, dental clinics and laboratories are incorporating these scanners into their practice to enhance the overall patient experience, increase productivity, and improve treatment outcomes.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards portable and handheld intraoral dental scanners, as they offer greater flexibility and convenience. Additionally, the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning in intraoral dental scanners is expected to drive market growth in the coming years, as these technologies can improve diagnostic capabilities and treatment planning.</p><p>In conclusion, the intraoral dental scanner market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period.
