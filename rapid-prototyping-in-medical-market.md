<p><p>Rapid prototyping has revolutionized the medical industry by offering efficient and cost-effective methods for developing medical devices and models. Stereolithography Apparatus (SLA) uses a UV laser to solidify liquid photopolymer into layers, Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) cuts and bonds layers of thin material together, Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) fuses powdered material with a laser, Three Dimension Printing (3DP) utilizes a binder to form layers, and Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) deposits melted thermoplastic material. These methods enable the quick production of accurate prototypes, aiding in creating customized medical devices and improving patient care.</p></p>
