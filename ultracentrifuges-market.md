<p><p>Ultracentrifuges are high-speed laboratory instruments used for separating and analyzing various substances. Analytical ultracentrifuges are primarily used for determining the size and shape of molecules in a solution, providing information about molecular weight and aggregation state. On the other hand, preparative ultracentrifuges are utilized for separating mixtures into their individual components based on their density and size. These types of ultracentrifuges are commonly employed in protein purification and isolation processes, allowing researchers to obtain pure samples for further analysis or use.</p></p>
