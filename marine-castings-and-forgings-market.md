<p><p>The marine castings and forgings market primarily consists of two segments: marine castings and marine forgings. Marine castings refer to the process of creating metal components by pouring molten metal into a mold and letting it solidify into the desired shape. These castings are commonly used in shipbuilding and other marine applications. On the other hand, marine forgings involve shaping metal by applying pressure to it through various forging techniques. Marine forgings are known for their superior strength and durability, ideal for withstanding the harsh conditions in the marine industry. Both marine castings and forgings play a critical role in the manufacturing of marine equipment.</p></p>
