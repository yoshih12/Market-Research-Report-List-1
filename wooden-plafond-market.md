<p><p>The Wooden Plafond market consists of four main types: Linear Wood, Grill Wood, Tiles and Panels Wood, and Custom Shape Wood. Linear Wood plafonds feature straight and narrow wooden strips laid out in a linear pattern. Grill Wood plafonds have wooden slats arranged in a grill-like fashion, allowing for airflow and ventilation. Tiles and Panels Wood plafonds are made up of wooden tiles or panels that are fitted together to create a cohesive ceiling surface. Custom Shape Wood plafonds are bespoke designs tailored to specific shapes and patterns as per customer requirements.</p></p>
