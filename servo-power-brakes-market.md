<p><p>The servo power brakes market consists of three main types: vacuum servo brakes, pneumatic servo brakes, and hydraulic servo brakes. Vacuum servo brakes use a vacuum source to assist the braking process, providing improved braking power. Pneumatic servo brakes utilize compressed air to enhance the braking force, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Hydraulic servo brakes utilize hydraulic pressure to boost the force applied to the brakes, ensuring efficient braking performance. These different types cater to various industries and applications, offering different levels of power and efficiency for braking systems.</p></p>
