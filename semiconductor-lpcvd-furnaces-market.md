<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the semiconductor LPCVD furnaces market, there are two types of furnace orientations: vertical and horizontal. Vertical LPCVD furnaces have a vertically oriented reaction chamber, where the wafers are stacked one on top of another. This design allows for higher throughput and is suitable for large-scale production. On the other hand, horizontal LPCVD furnaces feature a horizontally oriented reaction chamber, where wafers lie side by side. This design is more suitable for research and smaller-scale production, offering flexibility and ease of use.</p></p>
