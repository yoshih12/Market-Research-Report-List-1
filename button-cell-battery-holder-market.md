<p><strong>The Button Cell Battery Holder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Button Cell Battery Holder market can be categorized into two main types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. In the Surface Mount market, the battery holders are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB). This type of holder enables efficient space utilization and ease of manufacturing. On the other hand, the Through Hole market consists of holders that require leads to be inserted through holes in the PCB for secure mounting. These holders are generally more durable and provide better stability for the batteries.</p></p>
