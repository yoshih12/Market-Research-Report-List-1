<p><strong>The Fixed Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed thermal scanners market is categorized into three types: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners operate at a wavelength range of 1.4 to 3 microns, suitable for areas with high humidity or smoke. MWIR scanners function within a wavelength range of 3 to 5 microns, ideal for detecting small temperature variations. LWIR scanners operate at a wavelength of 8 to 14 microns, offering high sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for long-range detection and surveillance applications.</p></p>
