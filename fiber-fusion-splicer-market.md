<p><p>A fiber fusion splicer is a specialized device used to join two or more optical fibers together. Fusion splicing involves melting the ends of the fiber optic cables together, resulting in a seamless connection that minimizes signal loss and maintains high transmission quality.</p><p>The global fiber fusion splicer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
