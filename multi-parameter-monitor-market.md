<p><p>The Multi-Parameter Monitor market consists of two main types: General Ward Monitor and Surgery Monitor. General Ward Monitors are designed for continuous monitoring of patients' vital signs in regular hospital rooms. These monitors typically measure parameters such as heart rate, blood pressure, oxygen saturation, and temperature. Surgery Monitors, on the other hand, are specifically designed for operating rooms and provide advanced monitoring capabilities to ensure the safety and well-being of patients during surgical procedures. They may include additional features like invasive blood pressure monitoring and advanced arrhythmia detection.</p></p>
