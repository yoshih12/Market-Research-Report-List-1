<p><p>The Automotive Gesture Recognition Systems market is categorized into two types: Touch Based System and Touchless System. The Touch Based System utilizes touch sensors to register and interpret gestures made by users, while the Touchless System employs advanced technologies like cameras or infrared sensors to detect and interpret gestures without the need for physical contact. Both systems are used in automobiles to enable hands-free control of various features and improve safety by minimizing driver distraction.</p></p>
