<p><p>Auto glass encapsulation market types refer to the different materials used to seal the edges of automotive glass. TPE (thermoplastic elastomer), PUR (polyurethane), EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer), and PVC (polyvinyl chloride) are commonly used materials in this market. TPE is known for its flexibility and durability, while PUR offers strong adhesion properties. EPDM is resistant to weathering and ozone exposure, making it ideal for outdoor use. PVC is cost-effective and offers good insulation properties. Each material has unique characteristics that cater to different needs in the auto glass encapsulation market.</p></p>
