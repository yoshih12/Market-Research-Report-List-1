<p><strong>The Flap Barrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Movement</li><li>Double Movement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flap barrier market can be categorized into single movement and double movement types. Single movement flap barriers allow only one person to pass through at a time, ensuring controlled access. These barriers are commonly used in areas with low foot traffic. On the other hand, double movement flap barriers allow two people to pass through simultaneously in opposite directions, making them suitable for areas with higher foot traffic. These barriers provide efficient access control while managing a larger flow of people.</p></p>
