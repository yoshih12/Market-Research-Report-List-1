Conveyor dishwashers are designed for high-volume establishments, with a conveyor belt system that allows continuous cleaning. Door-type dishwashers are larger and feature a front-loading door, suitable for medium to large kitchens. These different types cater to various businesses' needs, providing options for efficient and effective washing of dishes in commercial settings.</p></p>
