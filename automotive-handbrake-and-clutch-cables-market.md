<p><p>The automotive handbrake and clutch cables market consist of two types: clutch cables and handbrake cables. Clutch cables are essential components that connect the clutch pedal to the clutch mechanism, allowing for the engagement and disengagement of the clutch. Handbrake cables, on the other hand, connect the handbrake lever to the parking brake mechanism, enabling the locking of the rear wheels to prevent the vehicle from rolling when parked. Both types of cables are crucial for the proper functioning of the clutch and handbrake systems in vehicles.</p></p>
