<p><p>A Spectral Interference Displacement Sensor is a non-contact measurement device used to determine the distance between the sensor and a target surface. It operates based on the principle of spectral interference, where a light beam is split into two paths, with one reflected off the target surface and the other reflected off a reference mirror. The resulting interference pattern is analyzed to accurately measure the distance.</p><p>The Spectral Interference Displacement Sensor Market is projected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
