<p><strong>The Steam Condenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jet Condenser</li><li>Surface Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steam condensers are devices used in power plants to convert steam back into water for reuse. There are two main types of steam condenser markets: jet condenser and surface condenser. </p><p>A jet condenser market involves using a high-pressure jet of cooling water to directly mix with the steam, leading to immediate condensation. It is commonly used in small power plants or where water availability is limited.</p><p>On the other hand, a surface condenser market uses a separate cooling medium, such as water or air, to indirectly extract heat from the steam, resulting in condensation and water collection in separate chambers. Surface condensers are typically utilized in large power plants, providing better efficiency and water conservation.</p></p>
