<p><p>Survival floats are classified into two main market types - foam survival floats and inflated survival floats. Foam survival floats are made of buoyant foam material, allowing them to stay afloat without any inflation required. They are lightweight, durable, and easy to use, making them popular for various water activities. On the other hand, inflated survival floats need to be blown up with air before use. They are typically compact, portable, and offer adjustable buoyancy levels, making them suitable for different water conditions and user preferences.</p></p>
