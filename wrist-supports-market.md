There are two main types of wrist supports available - those with thumb and those without thumb. Wrist supports with thumb include a separate section to support the thumb along with the wrist, offering extra stability and preventing thumb movement. On the other hand, wrist supports without thumb only cover the wrist area, providing compression and support without restricting thumb movement. Both types serve the purpose of protecting the wrist and promoting healing.</p></p>
