<p><p>Mechanical Absolute Encoders are devices used for the measurement and control of rotational motion. Singleturn Encoders are capable of tracking a full revolution and provide position feedback within one revolution. Multiturn Encoders, on the other hand, can track multiple revolutions and provide position feedback beyond one revolution. Singleturn Encoders are suitable for applications where precision within a single rotation is required, while Multiturn Encoders are ideal for applications requiring tracking and position feedback beyond a single revolution. These encoders cater to different needs in various industries, including automation, robotics, and manufacturing.</p></p>
