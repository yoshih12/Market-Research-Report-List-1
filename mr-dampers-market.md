The market for MR dampers can be categorized into active dampers and semi-active dampers. Active dampers dynamically adjust damping levels based on real-time conditions, providing optimal suspension control. Semi-active dampers offer limited adjustment capabilities and are often cost-effective alternatives to active dampers. Both types of dampers play a crucial role in enhancing ride comfort, vehicle stability, and reducing vibration in several applications, including automotive, aerospace, and civil engineering.</p></p>
