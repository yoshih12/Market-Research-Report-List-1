<p><p>Medical patient monitors are devices used in healthcare settings to track and record vital signs and other physiological parameters of patients. ECG/EKG monitors measure the electrical activity of the heart, helping diagnose heart conditions. Anesthesia monitors measure a patient's vital signs during surgery or anesthesia administration. Vital signs monitors measure various parameters like temperature, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation. Other types of monitors include fetal monitors, glucose monitors, and respiratory monitors, which monitor fetal heart rate, blood glucose levels, and respiratory functions, respectively. These monitors aid in patient assessment and ensuring their well-being during medical procedures.</p></p>
