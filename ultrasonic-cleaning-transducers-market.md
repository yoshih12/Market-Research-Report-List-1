These sound waves create microscopic bubbles in a liquid cleaning solvent, which implode upon contact with surfaces to remove contaminants. <p><ul><li>Screw Hole</li><li>Non-Screw Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the ultrasonic cleaning transducers market, there are two types: screw hole and non-screw hole transducers. Screw hole transducers have a threaded hole, allowing them to be easily screwed into the cleaning tank or system. This type is commonly used in industrial and commercial cleaning applications. On the other hand, non-screw hole transducers do not have a threaded hole and are typically bonded or mounted onto the cleaning tank. These transducers are often utilized in smaller-scale and customized cleaning systems.</p></p>
