<p><p>The Active Antenna Unit (AAU) is a crucial component in wireless communication systems. It is composed of an array of antennas, radio frequency (RF) components, and digital signal processing capabilities. The AAU is typically deployed in base stations and enables beamforming, which enhances network capacity, coverage, and spectral efficiency.</p><p>The Active Antenna Unit (AAU) Market is projected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The main drivers of this growth include the increasing demand for high-speed data connectivity, rising adoption of IoT devices, and the growing popularity of smart cities. Additionally, the deployment of 5G networks globally is expected to fuel the market growth for AAUs, as it requires more advanced and powerful antennas to handle the increased data traffic.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the AAU market is the integration of Massive MIMO (Multiple Input Multiple Output) technology. This technology uses numerous antennas at both the transmitter and receiver ends to enhance network performance. The integration of AAUs with Massive MIMO technology enables operators to increase capacity and coverage while reducing interference.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing a shift towards virtualized and software-defined AAUs. This trend allows for easier network deployment and management, as well as improved scalability and flexibility.
