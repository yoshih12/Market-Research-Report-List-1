<p><p>Loudspeaker subwoofers are classified into two main types - powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers have an inbuilt amplifier, making them a complete sound system on their own. They are more convenient to use and offer better control over the bass output. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to power them. They are generally more affordable and offer a wider range of customization options. both types cater to the demand for enhanced low-frequency sound reproduction in various audio applications.</p></p>
