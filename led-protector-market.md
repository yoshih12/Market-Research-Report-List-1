<p><p>The LED protector market can be classified into three main types: surface mount package, leaded package, and others. Surface mount package refers to LED protectors that are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, providing compactness and ease of installation. Leaded package protectors have protruding leads that allow for easy soldering onto the circuit board. The "others" category includes various packages that are not as commonly used as surface mount or leaded package protectors, such as chip-on-board or ball grid array packages.  Overall, these market types cater to different installation preferences and requirements.</p></p>
