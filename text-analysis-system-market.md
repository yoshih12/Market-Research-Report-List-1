<p><p>The text analysis system market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based text analysis systems operate through internet servers, allowing users to access and analyze large amounts of text data remotely. These systems offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premise text analysis systems are installed and maintained on local servers within the user's organization. These systems provide greater control, security, and customization options but require higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance.
