<p><p>Diaphragm seals are devices used to protect pressure measuring instruments from corrosive or viscous process media. Two common types of diaphragm seals in the market are low pressure and high pressure diaphragm seals. A low pressure diaphragm seal is designed to handle low-pressure applications, providing a barrier between the process media and the pressure instrument. On the other hand, a high pressure diaphragm seal is specifically designed to withstand high pressures and ensure accurate measurements in demanding environments. Both types serve the purpose of extending the lifespan and accuracy of pressure measuring instruments.</p></p>
