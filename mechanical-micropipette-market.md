<p><p>The mechanical micropipette market consists of two main types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel micropipettes are designed to transfer small volumes of liquids in a single channel at a time, making them suitable for applications requiring precision and accuracy. On the other hand, multi-channel micropipettes can transfer multiple samples simultaneously using multiple channels, enhancing efficiency and reducing repetitive pipetting tasks. Both types play a crucial role in research laboratories, clinical diagnostics, and other industries where precise liquid handling is essential.</p></p>
