<p><p>Magneto Optic Current Transformers (MOCT) are devices used to measure electric current using the magneto-optic effect. MOCTs are categorized into two types: fiber type and non-fiber type. </p><p>Fiber type MOCTs use an optical fiber to transmit the measurement signal, enabling long-distance transmission without loss or interference. They offer high accuracy and immunity to electromagnetic interference. On the other hand, non-fiber type MOCTs use a coil instead of an optical fiber, making them more compact and cost-effective. Both types provide efficient and reliable current measurements in different applications.</p></p>
