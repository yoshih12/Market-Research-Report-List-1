<p><p>The dry system valve market comprises two main types: flanged type and grooved type valves. Flanged type valves are designed with flanges at both ends, allowing them to be directly connected to pipes using bolts or welding. They are commonly used in high-pressure applications. On the other hand, grooved type valves feature grooved ends which can be easily connected using couplings, making them suitable for quick installation or disassembly. They are typically used in fire protection systems or in applications where frequent maintenance is required.</p></p>
