<p><p>The video drones market can be classified into three types: fixed-wing, rotary blade, and hybrid drones. Fixed-wing drones have a streamlined shape like an airplane and are typically used for long-range surveillance or mapping purposes. Rotary blade drones, also known as quadcopters, are the most popular type and are widely used for aerial photography and videography. Hybrid drones combine features of both fixed-wing and rotary blade drones, allowing them to hover like a quadcopter but also achieve longer flight times. Each type serves different purposes and caters to specific user needs.</p></p>
