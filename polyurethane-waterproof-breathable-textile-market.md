<p><p>The Polyurethane Waterproof Breathable Textile market includes three main types: Densely Woven, Coated, and Membrane. Densely Woven textiles are tightly knit to provide a barrier against water while allowing air circulation. Coated textiles have a layer of polyurethane applied to the surface for waterproofing. Membrane textiles have a thin layer of polyurethane sandwiched between layers for both waterproofing and breathability. Each type offers unique benefits and features for different applications in industries such as outdoor gear, sportswear, and medical textiles.</p></p>
