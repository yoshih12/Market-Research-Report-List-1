<p><p>Variable Data Printing (VDP) is a form of digital printing that enables customized documents to be printed with unique information. The Release Liner market in VDP refers to the use of a silicone-coated paper or film liner that protects the adhesive surface until the label is applied. This liner is then removed and discarded after application. On the other hand, the Linerless market eliminates the need for a liner by using a special adhesive that allows labels to be applied directly without any backing. Both of these techniques offer efficient and customizable printing solutions for various industries.</p></p>
