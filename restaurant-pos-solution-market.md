<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The restaurant POS (Point of Sale) solution market can be categorized into three types: cloud-based, on-premise, and hybrid solutions. Cloud-based solutions operate remotely and are managed by a third-party service provider, allowing users to access their data and manage operations online. On-premise solutions involve locally installed software and require the restaurant to manage and secure its own infrastructure. Hybrid solutions combine elements of both cloud-based and on-premise solutions, offering flexibility and customization opportunities while ensuring data accessibility and security.</p></p>
