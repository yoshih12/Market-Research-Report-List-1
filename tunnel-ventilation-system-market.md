<p><p>The Tunnel Ventilation System market offers various types of systems to ensure efficient air circulation within tunnels. Transverse Tunnel Ventilation involves the installation of fans perpendicular to the tunnel, which push air across it. Semi-Transverse Tunnel Ventilation uses a combination of both transverse and longitudinal fans for effective air movement. Longitudinal Ventilation employs fans placed parallel to the tunnel's direction to create a continuous flow of air. These systems aim to enhance air quality, remove pollutants, control smoke in case of emergencies, and maintain safe conditions within tunnels.</p></p>
