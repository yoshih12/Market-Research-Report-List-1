<p><p>The dairy protein ingredients market is categorized into several types. Milk protein concentrates/isolates are derived from milk and contain both casein and whey proteins. Whey protein concentrates/isolates are produced from whey and are high in protein content. Whey protein hydrolysates undergo partial hydrolysis, making them easier to digest. Casein/caseinates are derived from milk and are commonly used in food products as a functional ingredient. Each of these protein ingredients serves various purposes in the food industry and offers unique functional properties.</p></p>
