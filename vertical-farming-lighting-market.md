<p><p>Vertical farming lighting market categorizes lighting types into two main categories - Halogen and LED. Halogen lighting technology produces light by heating a tungsten filament, while LED lighting operates by passing an electric current through a semiconductor material. Halogen lights are known for their warm, yellowish glow and are commonly used in residential and commercial settings. On the other hand, LED lights are more energy-efficient, have a longer lifespan, and offer a wider range of customizable colors, making them popular for vertical farming applications where precise light wavelengths are needed for optimal plant growth.</p></p>
