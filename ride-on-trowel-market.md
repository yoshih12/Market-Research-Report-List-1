<p><p>The Ride-On Trowel market consists of two main types: Hydrostatic Ride-On Trowels and Mechanical Ride-On Trowels. Hydrostatic Ride-On Trowels are equipped with a hydraulic drive system, providing smoother operation and better control over the machine. On the other hand, Mechanical Ride-On Trowels rely on a mechanical drive system, which may be less expensive but could result in less precision and control. Both types of trowels have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications depending on the required level of performance and control.</p></p>
