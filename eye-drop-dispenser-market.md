<p><p>The eye drop dispenser market comprises two main types: multiple dose and single dose dispensers. Multiple dose dispensers are designed to hold and dispense a larger quantity of eye drops over a specific period. These dispensers usually come with a nozzle or dropper, allowing users to control the amount of drops they need. Single dose dispensers, on the other hand, contain pre-filled individual doses, eliminating the need for measurement and ensuring hygiene. These are typically single-use and discarded after each dose.</p></p>
