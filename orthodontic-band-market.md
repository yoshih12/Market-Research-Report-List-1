<p><p>Orthodontic bands are used in dental procedures to anchor orthodontic appliances and facilitate tooth movement. There are three types of bands available in the market: metal bands, silica gel bands, and ceramic bands. Metal bands, typically made of stainless steel, are strong and durable. Silica gel bands are soft and flexible, offering a comfortable fit. Ceramic bands, made of tooth-colored porcelain, are aesthetically pleasing and blend well with natural teeth. Each type of band has its advantages, catering to different patient preferences and treatment needs.</p></p>
