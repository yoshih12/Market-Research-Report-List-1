<p><p>Automated fluid dispensing systems include various types of equipment such as valves, pumps, and dispensers. Valves in these systems are used to control the flow of fluids by opening or closing passages. Pumps are utilized to regulate and move fluids from one place to another, providing the necessary pressure for the dispensing process. Dispensers, on the other hand, are designed to accurately measure and distribute fluids in specific quantities. These three components collectively contribute to the functioning of automated fluid dispensing systems.</p></p>
