<p><ul><li>Wired Leak Detection Equipment</li><li>Wireless Leak Detection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leak detection equipment market can be divided into two types: wired leak detection equipment and wireless leak detection equipment. Wired leak detection equipment consists of sensors that are connected to a central monitoring system through wired connections. It provides real-time monitoring of leaks in a fixed location. On the other hand, wireless leak detection equipment uses wireless technology to transmit data from sensors to the central monitoring system. It offers convenience and flexibility as it eliminates the need for complex wiring, making it suitable for monitoring leaks in remote or hard-to-reach areas.</p></p>
