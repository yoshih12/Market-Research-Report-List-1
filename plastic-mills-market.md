<p><ul><li>Horizontal Plastic Mills</li><li>Vertical Plastic Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic mills refer to the equipment used for efficiently processing plastic materials. Horizontal plastic mills are designed with a horizontal axis of rotation, where the material is fed from one end and processed through various mechanisms for size reduction and homogenization. On the other hand, vertical plastic mills have a vertical axis of rotation, with the material being fed from the top and processed downwards. Both types of mills are widely used in industries such as recycling, plastic manufacturing, and waste management, offering different advantages based on their design and capabilities.</p></p>
