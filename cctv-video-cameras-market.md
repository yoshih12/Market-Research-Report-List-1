<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>CCTV video cameras can be classified into three main types: analog cameras, IP cameras, and hybrid cameras. Analog cameras are traditional systems that record videos and transmit signals through coaxial cables. IP cameras, on the other hand, use the internet protocol to transmit data and video footage over an IP network, allowing remote access and higher resolution. Hybrid cameras combine the functionalities of both analog and IP cameras, providing the flexibility to migrate from analog to IP systems gradually. Each type caters to different surveillance needs and preferences.</p></p>
