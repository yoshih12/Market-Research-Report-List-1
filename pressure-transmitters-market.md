<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pressure Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Differential Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Multivariable Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure transmitters market includes various types of pressure transmitters. Absolute pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, while differential pressure transmitters measure the difference between two pressures. Gauge pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Lastly, multivariable transmitters measure multiple variables such as pressure, temperature, and flow. These different types of pressure transmitters are utilized in industries such as oil and gas, healthcare, automotive, and manufacturing, to accurately measure and monitor pressure in various applications.</p></p>
