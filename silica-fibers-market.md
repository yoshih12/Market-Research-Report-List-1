<p><p>The Silica Fibers market includes various types such as glass fibers, plastic fibers, and synthetically fused silica fibers. Glass fibers are made by drawing molten glass into thin strands, widely used in communication networks. Plastic fibers are made of polymers and are often used in short-range communication applications. Synthetically fused silica fibers are made by melting and drawing synthetic silica, offering high purity and excellent optical properties. These fibers find extensive applications in aerospace, medical devices, and telecommunications, meeting different requirements for strength, flexibility, and transmission capabilities in various industries.</p></p>
