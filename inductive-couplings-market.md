Axial market refers to inductive couplings used in applications where the primary and secondary winding are arranged concentrically along the same axis for efficient power transfer. Radial market, on the other hand, refers to couplings where the primary and secondary windings are arranged radially, generally in a circular or annular shape, enabling power transfer in multiple directions. These different market types cater to specific requirements and use cases in various industries.</p></p>
