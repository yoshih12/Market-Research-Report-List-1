The market for laser distance sensors can be categorized into different types, including digital laser sensors, CMOS laser sensors, and others. Digital laser sensors convert the received laser signal into digital output, offering high accuracy and fast response. CMOS laser sensors integrate a CMOS image sensor with laser diodes, providing high resolution and versatility. Other types of laser distance sensors may include analog laser sensors or special-purpose sensors designed for specific applications. Overall, laser distance sensors cater to diverse requirements for distance measurement.</p></p>
