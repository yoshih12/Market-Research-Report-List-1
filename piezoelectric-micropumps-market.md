<p><p>Piezoelectric micropumps are a type of miniature pump that utilize piezoelectric materials to generate controlled fluid flow. There are two main types in the market: piezoelectric air pumps and piezoelectric water pumps. Piezoelectric air pumps are designed to pump air or gases, while piezoelectric water pumps are used to pump liquids. Both types operate based on the piezoelectric effect, where the material expands or contracts when exposed to an electric field, leading to the generation of pressure and subsequent fluid flow.</p></p>
