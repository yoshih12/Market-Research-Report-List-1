<p><ul><li>EPL Services</li><li>EVPL Services</li><li>E-LAN Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carrier Ethernet services refer to the transportation of Ethernet data packets over a wide area network by telecommunication carriers. The market can be divided into three types: EPL services, EVPL services, and E-LAN services. EPL services, or Ethernet Private Line services, provide point-to-point connectivity between two locations. EVPL services, or Ethernet Virtual Private Line services, allow multiple locations to connect to each other, creating a virtual private network. E-LAN services, or Ethernet LAN services, enable multiple locations to communicate with each other in a multipoint-to-multipoint fashion, forming a virtual local area network.</p></p>
