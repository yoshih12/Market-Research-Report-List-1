<p><p>Dicing blades for wafer dicing machines are used in the semiconductor industry to cut semiconductor wafers into individual chips. There are two main types of dicing blades: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades have a metallic hub that provides support and stability during the cutting process. On the other hand, hubless dicing blades do not have a hub and rely on the blade's flange for support. Both types of blades have their advantages and are used in different applications within the wafer dicing process.</p></p>
