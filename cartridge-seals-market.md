<p><strong>The Cartridge Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Seals</li><li>Double Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cartridge seals are used in various industrial applications to prevent leakage between rotating equipment and stationary parts. Single seals are designed with one set of sealing faces, making them suitable for applications where low pressure or non-toxic fluids are involved. On the other hand, double seals consist of two sets of sealing faces, creating a barrier fluid between them. This configuration is used in applications where toxic, explosive, or abrasive fluids need to be sealed to ensure safety and prevent contamination. Both types of seals offer effective sealing solutions for different requirements in the market.</p></p>
