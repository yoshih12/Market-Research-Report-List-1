<p><p>Etch System market consists of various types, including Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP), Reactive Ion Etching (RIE), Deep Reactive Ion Etching (DRIE), and Others. ICP etching employs a high-frequency plasma source to etch materials, ensuring high precision and uniformity. RIE involves the use of reactive ions to remove material selectively from the surface, offering control and versatility in etching processes. DRIE is designed for deep and anisotropic etching in silicon wafers, enabling the production of complex micro-electromechanical systems. The "Others" category includes different etching techniques based on specific requirements or materials, providing diversified options and solutions to the market.</p></p>
