<p><p>Wafer wet processing systems are used in the semiconductor industry to clean, etch, and rinse wafers during the manufacturing process. There are three main types of these systems: semi-automatic, fully-automatic, and manual. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention, such as loading and unloading of wafers, while the cleaning and processing steps are automated. Fully-automatic systems are entirely automated, requiring minimal or no human intervention. Manual systems, on the other hand, rely entirely on manual labor for all processing steps, making them the least efficient option.</p></p>
