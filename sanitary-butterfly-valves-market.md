<p><p>Sanitary butterfly valves are categorized into two types: manual and actuated. Manual butterfly valves are operated by hand, where the user manually rotates the handle or lever to open or close the valve. Actuated butterfly valves are equipped with an actuator, which can be hydraulic, pneumatic, or electric. These valves are operated automatically, eliminating the need for manual intervention. Actuated butterfly valves offer greater precision, control, and ease of operation compared to manual valves. Both types are widely used in various industries for controlling and regulating fluid flow in sanitary applications.</p></p>
