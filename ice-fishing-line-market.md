The primary driver for this growth is the increasing popularity of ice fishing as a recreational activity in many regions around the world. Fluorocarbon lines are known for their low visibility underwater, high abrasion resistance, and excellent sensitivity. Anglers are increasingly switching to fluorocarbon lines for ice fishing due to their superior performance and effectiveness in icy conditions.</p><p>Another trend is the rising demand for pre-spooled ice fishing lines.
