<p><ul><li>Manual Rotary Laser Level</li><li>Self-leveling Rotary Laser Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Laser Level market consists of two major types: Manual Rotary Laser Levels and Self-leveling Rotary Laser Levels. Manual Rotary Laser Levels require manual adjustments to ensure the laser beam is accurately leveled. These models are more affordable and suitable for users with basic construction needs. On the other hand, Self-leveling Rotary Laser Levels have built-in mechanisms that automatically adjust the laser beam to ensure it remains level even on uneven surfaces. These models are more precise and convenient for advanced construction projects.</p></p>
