<p><p>Die cutting machinery is used to cut materials into specific shapes or patterns. There are three main types of die cutting machines: rotary die cutting machines, platen die cutting machines, and other machines.</p><p>Rotary die cutting machines use a cylindrical die that rotates to cut materials. They are efficient and commonly used for high-volume production.</p><p>Platen die cutting machines have a flat bed with a stationary die. They are suitable for thicker materials and precise cutting.</p><p>Other die cutting machines include laser die cutting machines, water jet die cutting machines, and digital die cutting machines. These machines use different cutting methods and are suitable for various materials and applications.</p></p>
