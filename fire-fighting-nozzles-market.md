One of the major trends is the development of innovative fire fighting nozzles with advanced features. These include the incorporation of flow control technology, adjustable flow patterns, and automatic pressure adjustment capabilities. These advancements enhance the effectiveness and efficiency of fire suppression operations.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing adoption of lightweight and compact fire fighting nozzles. Manufacturers are focusing on designing lightweight and ergonomic nozzles that can be easily handled by firefighters.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is also witnessing a growing demand for fire fighting nozzles that are compatible with various types of foam concentrates.
