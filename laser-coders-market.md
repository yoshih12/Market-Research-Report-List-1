<p><p>The Laser Coders Market consists of three major types: Fibre Laser Coder, CO2 Laser Coder, and Vanadate Laser Coder. Fibre Laser Coders use a solid-state laser diode to create a laser beam for coding purposes. CO2 Laser Coders utilize a carbon dioxide gas mixture for generating the laser beam. Vanadate Laser Coders employ vanadate crystals to produce a laser beam suitable for coding requirements. These laser coding technologies are widely used for high-quality marking and coding applications across various industries due to their precision, speed, and reliability.</p></p>
