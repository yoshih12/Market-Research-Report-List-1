<p><p>LMRS (Land Mobile Radio Systems) can operate within different frequency ranges, each with specific characteristics. The VHF market, ranging from 40MHz to 174MHz, offers efficient long-range communication and is commonly used in rural and open areas. The UHF market, from 200MHz to 512MHz, provides good signal penetration in buildings and urban environments. The SHF market, spanning from 700MHz to 1000MHz, offers high-speed data transfer capabilities suitable for applications requiring large bandwidth. These market types help in selecting appropriate LMRS frequencies based on specific geographical and communication needs.</p></p>
