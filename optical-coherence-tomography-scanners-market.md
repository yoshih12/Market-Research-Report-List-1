<p><p>Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) scanners are medical imaging devices that utilize light waves to generate detailed cross-sectional images of tissues and organs. There are different types of OCT scanners available in the market. </p><p>Spectral Domain (SD) OCT is a commonly used scanner that provides high-resolution images by analyzing the reflected light from the examined tissue. Swept Source (SS) OCT is another type that uses a different light source to penetrate deeper tissues, making it suitable for certain applications. </p><p>Apart from these, there are other niche market variants available that cater to specific requirements and preferences of medical professionals. These variants may offer unique features, enhanced imaging capabilities, or specialized applications to further expand the utility of OCT technology.</p></p>
