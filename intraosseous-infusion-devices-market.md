<p><p>Intraosseous infusion devices market consists of two main types: manual intraosseous infusion devices and automatic intraosseous infusion devices. Manual devices require manual insertion into the bone to deliver fluids or medications directly into the marrow, while automatic devices use a power-driven mechanism for easier and faster insertion. Both types serve the purpose of providing quick access to the vascular system for emergency treatment when intravenous access is challenging or not possible. These devices are essential in emergency medical situations such as trauma, cardiac arrest, or patients with difficult venous access.</p></p>
