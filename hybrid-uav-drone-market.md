Hybrid drones offer extended flight time, higher speed, and increased payload capacity, making them ideal for military operations.</p><p>In the agriculture sector, hybrid UAV drones are being used for crop monitoring, spraying pesticides, and collecting data for yield estimation, soil analysis, and irrigation management. The ability of hybrid drones to cover large agricultural areas quickly and accurately is driving their adoption in this sector.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the adoption of hybrid UAV drones in the media and entertainment industry for aerial filming and photography.
