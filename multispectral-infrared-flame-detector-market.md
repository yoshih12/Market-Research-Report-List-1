<p><p>A multispectral infrared flame detector is a device used to detect the presence of flames by sensing the infrared radiation emitted by them. This type of detector is commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, power generation, and aerospace, where the detection of flames is critical for safety and security purposes.</p><p>The market for multispectral infrared flame detectors is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing emphasis on fire safety regulations and the need for advanced flame detection technologies in various industries.
