<p><p>Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) is a powerful technique used in the field of biosensors, which enables the real-time analysis of molecular interactions on a sensor chip's surface. SPR measures changes in the refractive index of the sensor chip's surface caused by the binding of target molecules to immobilized ligands. This provides valuable information about the kinetics, affinity, and concentration of the binding events, making it a crucial tool for various applications, including drug discovery, proteomics, and environmental monitoring.</p><p>The Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for label-free detection methods, growing R&D investment in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries, and the rising focus on personalized medicine are the key factors driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in SPR technology, such as the development of high-throughput systems and improved sensor chips, are further fueling the market expansion.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Surface Plasmon Resonance Market is the integration of SPR with other techniques to enhance its capabilities. For instance, the combination of SPR with mass spectrometry allows for the analysis of complex samples with high sensitivity and selectivity. Similarly, the integration of microfluidics with SPR enables the miniaturization of assays, reducing the sample and reagent volume requirements.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing adoption of SPR in drug discovery and development is boosting the market growth.
