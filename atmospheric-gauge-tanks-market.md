<p><p>Atmospheric gauge tanks are widely used in industries to store and transport various liquids and gases. They come in different market types, namely single compartment and double compartment tanks. Single compartment tanks have a single storage area without any partition, making them suitable for storing large quantities of a single substance. On the other hand, double compartment tanks consist of two separate storage areas, allowing the simultaneous transportation of different substances or the separation of incompatible liquids or gases. Both types cater to specific storage and transportation needs and are chosen based on the substances being stored or transported.</p></p>
