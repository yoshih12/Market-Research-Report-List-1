<p><ul><li>Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS)</li><li>Optical Channel Monitoring (OCM)</li><li>Variable Optical Attenuators (VOAs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ROADM (Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer) module market consists of several types such as Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS), Optical Channel Monitoring (OCM), and Variable Optical Attenuators (VOAs). WSS enables the selection and routing of specific wavelengths in optical networks, while OCM allows for real-time monitoring of optical channels to ensure their quality. VOAs help regulate the power levels of optical signals, ensuring efficient transmission. All these modules play crucial roles in enabling flexible and efficient operations of optical networks.</p></p>
