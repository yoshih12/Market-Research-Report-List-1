<p><p>The Enterprise Data Center (EDC) market can be categorized into two types: Owned and Rented. In the Owned market, organizations build and maintain their own data centers, investing in the necessary infrastructure and managing the entire facility. On the other hand, in the Rented market, companies lease space and services from third-party data center providers, who operate and maintain the facility. While the Owned market grants organizations more control and customization options, the Rented market offers more flexibility and cost-effectiveness, as the maintenance burden falls on the service provider.</p></p>
