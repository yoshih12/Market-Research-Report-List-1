<p><strong>The Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Conversion FPDs</li><li>Direct Conversion FPDs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flat Panel Detector (FPD) market consists of two types: Indirect Conversion FPDs and Direct Conversion FPDs. Indirect Conversion FPDs capture X-ray images by using a scintillator that converts X-ray photons into light, which is then converted into electrical signals for image formation. Direct Conversion FPDs, on the other hand, directly convert X-ray photons into electrical charges, eliminating the need for a scintillator. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages in terms of image quality, cost, and performance in medical imaging applications.</p></p>
