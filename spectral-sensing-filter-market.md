<p><strong>The Spectral Sensing Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dichroic Filters</li><li>Neutral Density (ND) Filters</li><li>Band-pass Filters</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectral sensing filter market encompasses various types of filters used for different applications. Dichroic filters are designed to reflect certain wavelengths while allowing others to pass through. Neutral density (ND) filters reduce the intensity of light uniformly across a broad range of wavelengths. Band-pass filters allow only a specific range of wavelengths to pass through. There are also other types of filters available in the market, including linear variable filters, infrared cut-off filters, and longpass filters, each catering to specific spectral sensing requirements.</p></p>
