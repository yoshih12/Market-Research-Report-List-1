<p><strong>The Oxygen Nasal Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-flow Oxygen Nasal Cannula</li><li>High-flow Oxygen Nasal Cannula</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen nasal cannula market includes two types: low-flow and high-flow oxygen nasal cannulas. Low-flow nasal cannulas are designed to deliver a specific amount of supplemental oxygen to patients, typically up to 6 liters per minute. They are commonly used for patients with mild to moderate oxygen requirements. On the other hand, high-flow nasal cannulas deliver a higher flow rate of oxygen, often exceeding 6 liters per minute. They are utilized for patients with more severe oxygen needs or who require humidified oxygen therapy.</p></p>
