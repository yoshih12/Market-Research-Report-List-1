<p><p>Point of Care Technology refers to medical devices and technologies that are used at the patient's bedside or near the patient's point of care to provide immediate diagnostic results or access to medical information. Point of Care Technology includes devices such as handheld diagnostic devices, wearable sensors, mobile apps, and telehealth solutions.</p><p>The Point of Care Technology Market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to grow at a CAGR of 7.00% during the forecast period.
