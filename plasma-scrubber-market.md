<p><p>Plasma scrubber market types include oxygen-based plasma cleaning, hydrogen-based plasma cleaning, argon-based plasma cleaning, and others. Oxygen-based plasma cleaning involves the use of oxygen gas to create a plasma, which efficiently removes organic contaminants. Hydrogen-based plasma cleaning uses hydrogen gas to generate the plasma, proving effective for removing carbon-based materials and atomic hydrogen. Argon-based plasma cleaning employs argon gas to remove inorganic materials, such as metal oxides. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses alternative gases or mixture of gases used in the plasma cleaning process, providing unique cleaning properties based on specific requirements.</p></p>
