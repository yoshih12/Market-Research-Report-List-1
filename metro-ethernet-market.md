<p><strong>The Metro Ethernet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OVC</li><li>EVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metro Ethernet refers to the use of Ethernet technology in metropolitan area networks (MANs). In this market, there are two prevalent types: OVC (Ethernet Virtual Connection) and EVC (Ethernet Virtual Circuit). OVC refers to a point-to-point connection between two Ethernet ports, allowing for a dedicated connection. On the other hand, EVC supports multipoint connections, enabling multiple Ethernet ports to communicate within a single network. Both OVC and EVC offer flexible and scalable Ethernet solutions in the metro area, catering to different connectivity needs.</p></p>
