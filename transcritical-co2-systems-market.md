<p><p>Transcritical CO2 systems are advanced refrigeration systems that use carbon dioxide (CO2) as the refrigerant. These systems are widely used in commercial refrigeration, heat pump, and air conditioning applications. Unlike traditional refrigerants such as hydrofluorocarbons (HFCs), transcritical CO2 systems have a lower environmental impact and are considered more sustainable.</p><p>The current outlook of the transcritical CO2 systems market is highly positive. The market is driven by the growing demand for energy-efficient refrigeration systems due to increasing environmental concerns and stringent regulations regarding the use of fossil fuels. Moreover, the rising demand for natural refrigerants and advancements in technology are further propelling market growth.</p><p>The future of the transcritical CO2 systems market is expected to be promising.
