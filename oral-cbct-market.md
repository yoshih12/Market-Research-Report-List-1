The market for Oral CBCT is categorized into three types based on the size of the Field of View (FOV). Big FOV systems offer a wide scanning range for full maxillofacial imaging. Medium FOV systems have a slightly smaller scanning range, suitable for focused imaging of specific areas. Small FOV systems have the narrowest scanning range and are primarily used for single-tooth or localized imaging needs.</p></p>
