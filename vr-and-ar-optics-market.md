<p><p>The VR and AR optics market consists of two main types: waveguide and lens. Waveguide technology uses thin transparent materials to guide light, allowing for the creation of compact and lightweight headsets. On the other hand, lens technology involves the use of optical lenses to project virtual or augmented reality content directly into the user's eyes. Both waveguides and lenses play crucial roles in improving the visual experience, enhancing immersion, and minimizing the weight and size of the devices used in the virtual and augmented reality industry.</p></p>
