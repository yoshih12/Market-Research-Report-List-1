<p><p>Water fed poles are tools used in the cleaning industry to wash windows and other surfaces at height. They consist of a telescopic pole connected to a brush head, with water flowing through the pole and out of the brush head. This method eliminates the need for ladders or lifts, making it safer and more efficient for window cleaning tasks.</p><p>The water fed poles market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for efficient and safe cleaning techniques. The technology offers several advantages over traditional window cleaning methods, such as reduced risk of falls, improved reach, and faster cleaning times. These factors have contributed to the rising adoption of water fed poles in the cleaning industry.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the water fed poles market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period.
