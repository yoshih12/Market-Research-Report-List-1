<p><p>Vibration Infrared Detectors market includes two types: PC (Pyroelectric) and PV (Piezoelectric). PC detectors operate based on pyroelectric materials that generate a voltage when subjected to temperature changes caused by vibration. These detectors are used in various applications like motion sensing, security systems, and energy conservation. PV detectors, on the other hand, utilize piezoelectric materials that produce electric charge when subjected to mechanical stress caused by vibration. PV detectors find applications in areas such as vibration monitoring, structural health monitoring, and industrial machinery maintenance. Both types contribute to the growth and advancement of the Vibration Infrared Detectors market.</p></p>
