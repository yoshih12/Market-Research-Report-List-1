<p><p>The ANSI Centrifugal Pumps market consists of two main types: Horizontal ANSI Centrifugal Pumps and Vertical ANSI Centrifugal Pumps. Horizontal pumps are designed with the shaft and impeller in a horizontal position, making them suitable for applications where space is limited. Vertical pumps, on the other hand, have a vertical shaft and are commonly used in situations requiring higher flow rates or when the pump is submerged. Both types serve different purposes in the market and cater to specific needs based on the application requirements.</p></p>
