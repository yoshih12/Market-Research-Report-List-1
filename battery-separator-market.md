<p><p>A battery separator is a permeable membrane that is placed between the anode and cathode of a battery to prevent short circuits while allowing the flow of ions. The main function of a battery separator is to prevent direct contact between the positive and negative electrodes, which could cause a short circuit and lead to battery failure or even explosions.</p><p>The future outlook for the battery separator market is promising. The increasing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and portable electronic devices is driving the growth of the battery market, which in turn is boosting the demand for battery separators.
