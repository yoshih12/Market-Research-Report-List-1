<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The market for Mercury Analyzers is classified into two types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer. A Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer uses a heated chamber to convert mercury into vapor, which is then analyzed using an absorption technique. On the other hand, a Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer relies on the emission of fluorescent light when mercury atoms are excited by a radiation source. Both types are utilized in various industries to detect and measure mercury levels accurately.</p></p>
