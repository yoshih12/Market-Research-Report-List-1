The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable packaging solutions. The growing awareness among consumers regarding the environmental impact of plastic packaging has led to a shift towards paper-based packaging alternatives. Liquid gable top cartons provide an eco-friendly option that is biodegradable, recyclable, and made from renewable resources. </p><p>Moreover, the rising consumption of liquid products, especially in developing countries, is also fueling the demand for liquid gable top cartons.
