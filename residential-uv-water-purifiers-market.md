<p><p>Residential UV Water Purifiers are categorized into two types: Horizontal and Vertical. Horizontal Residential UV Water Purifiers are designed to be placed horizontally on a surface, such as a countertop or under a sink. They provide effective purification by utilizing ultraviolet technology to eliminate harmful bacteria, viruses, and microorganisms from water. On the other hand, Vertical Residential UV Water Purifiers are designed to be mounted vertically on walls, making them more space-efficient. Both types of purifiers offer the same level of water purification using UV technology, but differ in their design and placement options.</p></p>
