<p><p>Nonmetallic gaskets are classified into three main types based on the materials used: Elastomeric Gaskets, which are made of rubber or plastic and offer good flexibility and sealing properties; Advanced Fiber-Based Gaskets, which are manufactured using materials like aramid fibers and offer high temperature and chemical resistance; and Plastic-Based Gaskets, which are typically made of materials like PTFE and provide excellent resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. Each type of gasket is designed for specific applications based on their unique properties and characteristics.</p></p>
