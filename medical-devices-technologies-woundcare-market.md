Transparent film dressings are waterproof and allow for easy monitoring of the wound. Hydrofiber dressings are highly absorbent and form a gel when in contact with exudate. Hydrogels provide a moist healing environment. Collagen dressings promote wound healing by providing a scaffold for cell growth.
