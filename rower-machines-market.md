<p><p>Rower machines are fitness equipment that simulate the motion of rowing a boat, providing a full-body workout. There are four main types of rower machines in the market. Magnetic rowers use magnetic resistance for a smooth, quiet operation. Air rowers use the resistance from a flywheel, creating a dynamic and challenging workout. Water rowers provide a realistic rowing experience by utilizing a water-filled tank for resistance. Hydraulic rowers use hydraulic pistons to generate resistance and are often more compact and affordable. Each type has unique features suitable for different preferences and fitness goals.</p></p>
