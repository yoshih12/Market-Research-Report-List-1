<p><p>The semiconductor wet etching equipment market can be categorized into different types based on the materials being etched. Dielectric etching equipment is used to selectively remove dielectric materials, such as silicon dioxide or silicon nitride, from the semiconductor surface. Silicon etching equipment is used to remove or pattern silicon layers. Metal etching equipment is used to etch various metal layers on the semiconductor surface. Additionally, there are other types of wet etching equipment available for specific applications, such as organic material etching or compound semiconductor etching.</p></p>
