<p><p>Nano-porous materials are categorized into various types such as zeolites, silica gel, activated carbon, activated alumina, and others. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates with uniform pores, silica gel is a highly porous form of silicon dioxide, activated carbon has a large surface area due to its pores, while activated alumina is used for dehydration and purification. Other types of nano-porous materials include metal-organic frameworks and carbon nanotubes. These materials are utilized in various industries for applications such as gas separation, water purification, and catalysis.</p></p>
