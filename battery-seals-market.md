<p><p>The battery seals market consists of three main types: bonded seals, compressive seals, and others. Bonded seals are created by bonding two materials together to form a tight seal. Compressive seals are designed to provide a secure fit by compressing the seal against the battery. These seals play a crucial role in preventing leakage and ensuring the optimal performance and safety of batteries.</p></p>
