<p><p>Automatic door sensors are used to detect the presence of individuals and open doors automatically, improving accessibility and convenience. Microwave sensors operate by emitting high-frequency radio waves that bounce off objects and are detected when the waves return. This type of sensor is highly precise and is suitable for detecting any movement in the vicinity. Infrared sensors function by emitting infrared light beams and sensing when they are interrupted by someone or something. These sensors are economical and commonly found in a variety of automatic door systems.</p></p>
