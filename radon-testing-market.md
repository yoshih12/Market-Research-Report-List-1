<p><ul><li>Radon Detectors</li><li>Radon Test kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radon testing market includes two main types of products: radon detectors and radon test kits. Radon detectors are electronic devices that continuously monitor and measure the amount of radon gas in indoor spaces. They provide real-time data and are often used by professionals for long-term monitoring. On the other hand, radon test kits are passive devices that collect air samples over a specific period of time. These kits are simple to use and typically sent to laboratories for analysis, providing homeowners with a snapshot of radon levels in their homes.</p></p>
