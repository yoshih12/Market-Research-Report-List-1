<p><p>The Agricultural Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) market consists of two main types: Fixed Wing and Multi-Rotor. Fixed Wing UAVs are designed like airplanes with fixed wings and require a runway for takeoff and landing. They have a longer flight range, higher payload capacity, and are more suitable for large-scale agricultural operations. On the other hand, Multi-Rotor UAVs, like drones, use multiple rotors to fly and can take off and land vertically. They are more maneuverable, easier to operate, and suitable for smaller farms or precision farming tasks.</p></p>
