<p><p>Ultrasonic corner sensors come in three main types: front corner sensors, back corner sensors, and side corner sensors. Front corner sensors are positioned at the front of a vehicle to assist with parking and avoiding collisions while moving forward. Back corner sensors are placed at the back of a vehicle and help with reversing safely. Side corner sensors are installed on the sides of a vehicle to aid in maneuvering through tight spaces and detecting obstacles in blind spots. Each type serves a specific purpose in enhancing the overall safety and convenience of driving.</p></p>
