<p><p>The robotic pool cleaner market can be divided into two main types: Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive. Crawler Drive cleaners use tracks or continuous treads to move around the pool, providing better traction and maneuverability in irregularly shaped pools or on walls and stairs. Wheel Drive cleaners, on the other hand, use wheels to move around the pool, offering faster speed and efficiency in large, square pools. Both types are popular choices for automatic pool cleaning due to their effectiveness and ease of use.</p></p>
