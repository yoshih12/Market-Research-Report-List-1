<p><p>The osmium sputtering target market consists of two main types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. In the rotary type market, the targets have a rotating mechanism which ensures uniform erosion during sputtering. This type is commonly used in applications where higher deposition rates are required, such as thin film production. On the other hand, the non-rotatable type market offers fixed targets that do not rotate. These targets are typically used in processes where higher accuracy and uniformity in film thickness are important, such as microelectronic fabrication.</p></p>
