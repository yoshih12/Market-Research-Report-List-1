<p><ul><li>Open Hole Applications</li><li>Cased Hole Applications</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slickline trucks offer various applications for both open hole and cased hole operations. In open hole applications, these trucks are used to perform tasks like logging, setting plugs, and taking samples in wells before they are cased. On the other hand, in cased hole applications, slickline trucks are used to perform tasks such as perforating, setting or retrieving downhole tools, and wellbore cleanouts in wells that have already been cased. These versatile trucks play a crucial role in the oil and gas industry by optimizing well operations and ensuring efficiency.</p></p>
