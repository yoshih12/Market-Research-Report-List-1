<p><p>The egg protein powder market is comprised of various types, including whole egg powder, egg white powder, and egg yolk powder. Whole egg powder is made by spray-drying whole eggs, preserving both the egg white and yolk. Egg white powder is produced by removing the yolk and dehydrating the egg white, while egg yolk powder is made solely from the yolk. These powdered forms offer convenient and versatile alternatives to fresh eggs, commonly used in baking, cooking, and as a protein supplement in various dietary and fitness routines.</p></p>
