<p><p>The cordless soldering iron market can be categorized into two types: externally soldering iron and internal soldering iron. Externally soldering irons are designed to be used in conjunction with an external power source, such as a battery pack or power supply. These irons offer high performance and versatility but may require additional equipment. In contrast, internal soldering irons have a built-in power source, usually a rechargeable battery. They are portable and convenient, allowing for easy soldering without the need for external power, making them ideal for on-the-go or remote usage.</p></p>
