<p><p>The handheld illuminometer market consists of two main types: digital illuminometers and analog illuminometers. Digital illuminometers utilize electronic sensors to measure and display light intensity readings in a numerical format on a digital screen. They offer precise and accurate measurements, making them popular in industries where precise lighting conditions are necessary. On the other hand, analog illuminometers use a traditional needle or dial system to indicate light intensity. While they may lack the precise measurements of digital illuminometers, they are often more cost-effective and simpler to use, making them suitable for less demanding applications.</p></p>
