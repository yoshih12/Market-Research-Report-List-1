<p><p>Reflow Oven Market Types refer to the different categories of ovens used in the manufacturing industry for the reflow soldering process. Convection ovens utilize forced hot air to heat the components uniformly, providing efficient heat transfer. They are widely used due to their flexibility and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, Vapor Phase Oven market refers to ovens that use a boiling liquid to transfer heat by condensation. These ovens offer precise and controlled soldering processes with low thermal stress, making them suitable for delicate and complex electronic assemblies.</p></p>
