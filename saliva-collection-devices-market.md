<p><ul><li>Whole Saliva Collection Devices</li><li>Glandular Saliva Collection Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Saliva collection devices are categorized into two types: whole saliva collection devices and glandular saliva collection devices. Whole saliva collection devices are designed to collect and store all the saliva produced by an individual. On the other hand, glandular saliva collection devices are specifically designed to collect saliva from specific salivary glands, such as parotid or submandibular glands. These devices are used in various medical and research purposes, including studying salivary biomarkers, diagnosing diseases, and monitoring oral health.</p></p>
