<p><p>Earth Leakage Circuit Breakers (ELCB) are safety devices installed in electrical systems to protect against electric shocks and fires caused by electrical faults. The market for ELCBs is divided into two types: Voltage Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (VELCB) and Current Earth Leakage Circuit Breaker (CELCB). VELCBs measure the voltage difference between the live and neutral wires, while CELCBs detect the current imbalance between the live and neutral wires. Both types work by automatically cutting off the power supply when leakage current exceeds a predetermined threshold, ensuring safety in electrical installations.</p></p>
