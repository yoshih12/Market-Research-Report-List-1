<p><ul><li>Microscopic Techniques</li><li>Macroscopic Techniques</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellular imaging market includes microscopic and macroscopic techniques. Microscopic techniques involve the use of high-resolution microscopes to visualize cells at a microscopic level, allowing detailed analysis of cellular structures and functions. This includes techniques like fluorescence microscopy, confocal microscopy, and electron microscopy. On the other hand, macroscopic techniques involve imaging larger structures or whole organisms using various imaging modalities such as computed tomography (CT), magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), and positron emission tomography (PET). These techniques provide a broader view of cellular behavior within the context of a larger organism or tissue.</p></p>
