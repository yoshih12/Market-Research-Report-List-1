<p><strong>The Ferrite Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Probe</li><li>Diameter Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ferrite Meter Market consists of two types of probes: the Area Probe and the Diameter Probe. The Area Probe is used to measure the ferrite content in a specific area of a material, providing accurate and localized readings. On the other hand, the Diameter Probe measures the ferrite content across the diameter of a material, giving a more comprehensive analysis. Both types of probes are important in industries that use ferrite-containing materials, such as power generation and transmission, automotive, and aerospace, as they help ensure quality control during manufacturing processes.</p></p>
