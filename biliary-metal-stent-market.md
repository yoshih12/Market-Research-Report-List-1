<p><p>A biliary metal stent is a medical device designed to treat obstructions in the biliary system, which includes the bile ducts and gallbladder. It is commonly used to relieve blockages caused by conditions such as gallstones, tumors, or strictures. The stent is placed during a minimally invasive procedure called endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP), where it helps to restore the normal flow of bile.</p><p>The biliary metal stent market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the rising prevalence of biliary disorders, increasing adoption of minimally invasive procedures, and advancements in stent technology. The market is also driven by the growing geriatric population, as they are more susceptible to biliary obstructions.</p><p>Additionally, the market is experiencing various trends that are shaping its growth. One of the significant trends is the development of fully covered self-expandable metal stents (FCSEMS) that offer advantages such as reduced risk of tissue ingrowth, improved patient comfort, and easier removal. These stents are gaining popularity among healthcare professionals and patients alike.</p><p>Another trend is the introduction of novel stent designs and materials, such as biodegradable stents. These stents have the potential to overcome some of the limitations associated with traditional metal stents, such as the need for stent removal procedures. Biodegradable stents are under extensive research and hold promise for the future of biliary stent treatments.</p><p>In conclusion, the biliary metal stent market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of biliary disorders, rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures, and advancements in stent technology. Furthermore, the market is witnessing trends such as the development of FCSEMS and the introduction of novel stent designs and materials.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Biliary Metal Stent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Covered Version</li><li>Non-Covered Version</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Biliary Metal Stent market offers two types of stents - Covered Version and Non-Covered Version. Covered Version stents have a layer that prevents the stent from growing into the surrounding tissue, reducing the risk of complications. These stents are commonly used for treating benign biliary strictures. On the other hand, Non-Covered Version stents lack this additional layer, making them suitable for use in malignant biliary strictures where ingrowth acts as a barrier for tumor growth. Both versions cater to specific medical conditions and have distinct advantages based on the patient's needs.</p></p>
