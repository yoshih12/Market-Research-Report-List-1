<p><p>Hydraulic insertion machines are used for inserting or pressing different materials together in various industries. There are three types of machines available in the market. Manual machines require manual operation for insertion processes. Semi-automatic machines combine manual and automatic functions, with operators controlling some aspects while the machine handles others. Fully automatic machines are operated entirely by the machine, requiring minimal human intervention. These different types cater to different production needs and offer varying levels of efficiency, speed, and precision.</p></p>
