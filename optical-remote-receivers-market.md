<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Optical Remote Receivers Market comprises two main types: Active Receiving Type and Passive Receiving Type. Active Receiving Type receivers utilize photodiodes to convert optical signals into electrical ones. These receivers are capable of amplifying weak signals and are commonly used in long-distance communication systems. In contrast, Passive Receiving Type receivers do not require power to operate and rely on the energy of the incoming optical signal to generate an electrical output. These receivers are suitable for short-distance applications where signal amplification is not necessary.</p></p>
