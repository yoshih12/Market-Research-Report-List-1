<p><p>The non-contact optical thickness gauge market includes different types based on the light sources used. Halogen light source gauges utilize a halogen bulb to emit light, which is then reflected and detected by the gauge to measure the thickness of an object. On the other hand, infrared LD (laser diode) gauges employ an infrared laser diode to emit light that is reflected back and measured to determine the thickness of the object. These different types cater to various industries and applications requiring accurate and non-destructive thickness measurements.</p></p>
