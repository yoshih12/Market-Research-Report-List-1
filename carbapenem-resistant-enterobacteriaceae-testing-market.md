The Modified Hodge CRE testing detects phenotypic expression of the carbapenemase enzyme, Minimal Inhibitory Concentration CRE testing assesses whether strains exhibit resistance to specific carbapenem antibiotics. Other types of CRE testing may include molecular methods or rapid diagnostic tests. These tests help in identifying CRE infections and guiding appropriate treatment strategies to overcome antibiotic resistance.</p></p>
