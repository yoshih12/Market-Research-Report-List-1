<p><p>The ductless heating and cooling split system market can be categorized into three types. The first is the mini split system, which consists of one outdoor unit connected to one indoor unit and is suitable for single-zone applications. The second type is the multi-split system, where one outdoor unit is connected to multiple indoor units, making it ideal for multi-zone applications. Lastly, the VRF (Variable Refrigerant Flow) system market involves one outdoor unit connected to multiple indoor units, allowing for precise temperature control in different zones and highly efficient operation.</p></p>
