<p><p>The Groove Milling Tools Market offers two types of tools - External Grooving and Internal Grooving. External Grooving tools are designed to create grooves on the outer surface of a workpiece. They are commonly used in applications where an external groove is required for components like O-rings or snap rings. On the other hand, Internal Grooving tools are designed to create grooves on the inner surface of a workpiece. They are commonly used in applications where an internal groove is needed for components like circlips or retaining rings.</p></p>
