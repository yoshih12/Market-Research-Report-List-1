<p><p>Bulk material handling equipment refers to machines used to transport and handle large quantities of materials. Stacking and loading equipment are designed for efficiently stacking and loading bulk materials onto trucks, railcars, or storage areas. This equipment includes stackers, reclaimers, and loaders. On the other hand, bulk conveying equipment is used to transport materials from one location to another within a facility. Examples include conveyor belts, screw conveyors, and bucket elevators. Both types of equipment help improve productivity and reduce manual labor in industries such as mining, construction, and agriculture.</p></p>
