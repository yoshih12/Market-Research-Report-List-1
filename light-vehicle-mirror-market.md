<p><p>The light vehicle mirror market includes two types of mirrors: internal mirrors and external mirrors. Internal mirrors are installed inside the vehicle and provide the driver with a rear-facing view of the road. They allow the driver to monitor the vehicles behind them and make safe lane changes. External mirrors, on the other hand, are mounted on the exterior of the vehicle. They provide a wider view of the surrounding road and help the driver to navigate traffic, park, and maintain awareness of their surroundings.</p></p>
