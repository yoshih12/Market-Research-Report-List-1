<p><strong>The Wafer Ring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dicing Wafer Ring</li><li>UV Film Wafer Ring</li><li>Fixed Wafer Ring</li><li>Stick Wafer Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for wafer rings can be classified into four types. Firstly, dicing wafer rings are used in the semiconductor industry for cutting wafers into individual chips. Secondly, UV film wafer rings are used to protect the wafers from contamination during the UV exposure process. Thirdly, fixed wafer rings are non-removable fixtures used to hold the wafers in place during various processes. Lastly, stick wafer rings are adhesive-backed and provide temporary adhesion to hold wafers during handling or transportation. These different types cater to specific requirements in wafer manufacturing and processing.</p></p>
