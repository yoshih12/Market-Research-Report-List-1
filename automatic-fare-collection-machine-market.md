<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Fare Collection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ticket Vending Machines (TVM)</li><li>Ticket Office Machines (TOM)</li><li>Add Value Machines (AVM)</li><li>Automatic Gate Machine (AGM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for automatic fare collection machines can be classified into different types. Ticket Vending Machines (TVM) are primarily used for purchasing tickets. Ticket Office Machines (TOM) are larger machines located at ticket offices, providing multiple services like ticket printing and payment. Add Value Machines (AVM) allow users to add value to their existing fare cards. Automatic Gate Machines (AGM) are gate barriers equipped with ticket readers, allowing entry or exit based on valid tickets. All these machines contribute to the efficient management of ticketing and payments in transportation systems.</p></p>
