<p><p>A TIG Torch (Tungsten Inert Gas Torch), also known as a GTAW (Gas Tungsten Arc Welding) Torch, is a type of welding torch used in the TIG welding process. TIG welding is a high-quality welding process that uses a non-consumable tungsten electrode to create the weld. The TIG Torch is responsible for emitting the arc and shielding gas to protect the weld from contamination.</p><p>The TIG Torch Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period (2021-2026), with a projected CAGR of 9.8%. <p><p>The TIG Torch market consists of two primary types: Handheld TIG Torches and Automatic TIG Torches. On the other hand, Automatic TIG Torches are designed to function autonomously with programmable settings. They are commonly used in high-volume production environments, where automation and efficiency are crucial.
