<p><p>Coronary artery disease therapeutics market offers various types of treatments for managing the condition. Beta-blockers are medications that slow down the heart rate and reduce blood pressure. Calcium channel blockers help relax and widen blood vessels to improve blood flow. Nitrates are drugs that dilate blood vessels to relieve chest pain. ACE inhibitors lower blood pressure by inhibiting an enzyme that narrows blood vessels. The "Others" category may include additional medications like antiplatelet agents or cholesterol-lowering drugs. All of these treatments aim to alleviate the symptoms and prevent complications of coronary artery disease.</p></p>
