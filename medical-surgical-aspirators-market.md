<p><p>The Medical Surgical Aspirators market includes two types of devices: Wall Mounted Suction Units and Portable Surgical Aspirators. Wall Mounted Suction Units are fixed to a wall and provide continuous suction through a vacuum system, commonly used in hospitals and surgical centers. Portable Surgical Aspirators, on the other hand, are compact and lightweight devices that can be easily carried and used in different settings, allowing for flexibility and mobility in medical procedures. Both types serve the purpose of removing fluid or debris from the body during surgical or medical procedures.</p></p>
