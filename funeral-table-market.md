<p><p>The funeral table market can be divided into two types: on casters and casterless. Funeral tables on casters are equipped with wheels, allowing for easy mobility and transportation within funeral homes. They enable convenient rearrangement of the table for various funeral proceedings. On the other hand, casterless funeral tables do not have wheels and are typically more stationary. They provide stability and a fixed position, suitable for specific requirements during funeral ceremonies. These different types cater to the diverse needs and preferences of funeral service providers.</p></p>
