<p><p>Lock washers are hardware components that are used to prevent nuts and bolts from loosening due to vibrations or other external forces. There are different types of lock washers available in the market. Internal star lock washers have teeth on the inside that bite into the mating surface, offering a secure grip. External star lock washers have teeth on the outside that grip the material against the nut or bolt head. Split lock washers are designed with a split ring that creates tension when compressed, preventing loosening. Other types include toothed lock washers, wave washers, and serrated lock washers, each offering unique benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
