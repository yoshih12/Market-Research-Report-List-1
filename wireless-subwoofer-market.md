<p><p>The wireless subwoofer market can be categorized into three types: active subwoofers, passive subwoofers, and other markets. </p><p>Active subwoofers have built-in amplifiers that power the subwoofer independently. They are easier to set up and offer more flexibility in terms of placement since they only require a power source.</p><p>Passive subwoofers, on the other hand, rely on an external amplifier to power the subwoofer. They are generally larger and require more complex installation but can provide higher power output.</p><p>Other markets in the wireless subwoofer industry include specialty subwoofers designed for specific applications, such as in-car subwoofers or outdoor subwoofers for events and concerts. These markets cater to specific needs and may have unique features or configurations.</p></p>
