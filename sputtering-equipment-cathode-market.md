<p><p>Sputtering equipment cathode market types can be classified as linear and circular. Linear cathodes are long and narrow in shape, enabling them to deposit material uniformly across a large substrate. They are commonly used in applications where a consistent coating thickness is required, such as in the production of semiconductors or solar cells. On the other hand, circular cathodes have a round shape and are used when a concentrated deposition of material is needed, such as in the production of optical coatings or magnetic storage devices.</p></p>
