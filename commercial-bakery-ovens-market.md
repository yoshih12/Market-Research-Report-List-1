<p><p>Commercial bakery ovens can be categorized into different types, such as convection oven and deck oven. Convection ovens use a fan to circulate hot air, ensuring even baking and quick cooking times. They are popular for their energy efficiency and versatility. On the other hand, deck ovens are known for their ability to produce high-quality artisan breads and pastries. They have multiple levels or decks that provide optimal heat distribution for consistent baking results. Both types of ovens are commonly used in commercial bakeries to meet various baking needs.</p></p>
