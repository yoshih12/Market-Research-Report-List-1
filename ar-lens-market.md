<p><p>The AR Lens market can be divided into three types based on Field of View (FOV): FOV 15°, FOV 23-30°, and FOV 40°. FOV refers to the extent of the virtual environment that a user can see through the lens. FOV 15° lenses offer a narrow field of view, restricting the virtual content to a limited area. FOV 23-30° lenses provide a slightly wider view, enhancing the immersive experience. FOV 40° lenses offer the widest field of view, allowing users to perceive a larger virtual environment. These different FOV options cater to varying user preferences and application requirements in the AR Lens market.</p></p>
