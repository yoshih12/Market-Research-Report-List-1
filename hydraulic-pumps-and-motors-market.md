<p><p>The hydraulic pumps and motors market comprises various types of products such as hydraulic gear pumps, hydraulic vane pumps, hydraulic piston pumps, hydraulic gear motors, hydraulic vane motors, and hydraulic piston motors. Hydraulic gear pumps operate by using rotating gears to generate hydraulic flow. Hydraulic vane pumps utilize vanes that slide in and out of the rotor to create flow. Hydraulic piston pumps rely on reciprocating pistons inside cylinders to generate hydraulic force. The same principles apply to hydraulic gear motors, hydraulic vane motors, and hydraulic piston motors, but these devices function as motors rather than pumps.</p></p>
