<p><p>The automotive connector terminals market can be categorized into two main types: pre-insulated forms and non-insulated forms. Pre-insulated forms refer to terminals that come with a predetermined insulating cover, making them ready to use. On the other hand, non-insulated forms are terminals without any built-in insulation, requiring additional insulation to be manually applied. Both types serve the same purpose of connecting electrical wires in automotive applications, but the difference lies in the presence or absence of insulation.</p></p>
