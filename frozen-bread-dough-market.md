<p><p>The frozen bread dough market consists of two types - fermented and unfermented. Fermented dough undergoes a natural process where yeast ferments the dough, enhancing its flavor, texture, and aroma. This type is popular for making traditional bread products. On the other hand, unfermented dough is not yeasted, resulting in a simpler taste and shorter preparation time. It is often used for making items like pizza crusts or rolls. The market for frozen bread dough offers both options to cater to diverse consumer preferences and culinary needs.</p></p>
