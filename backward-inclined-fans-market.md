<p><p>Backward inclined fans are categorized into two main types: single-width backward tilt fans and double-width backward fans. </p><p>Single-width backward tilt fans are compact and suitable for various applications with limited space. They feature blades that are inclined backward, which allows for increased efficiency, reduced noise, and improved airflow performance. These fans are commonly used in HVAC systems, cleanrooms, and industrial ventilation.</p><p>On the other hand, double-width backward fans have wider dimensions and are specifically designed for applications with higher airflow requirements. They offer increased capacity and can handle larger volumes of air. These fans are commonly utilized in industrial processes, power generation plants, and large commercial buildings.</p></p>
