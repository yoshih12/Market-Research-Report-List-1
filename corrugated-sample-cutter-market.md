<p><strong>The Corrugated Sample Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated sample cutter market is divided into two types - pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic sample cutters operate using compressed air and are known for their high precision and speed. They are suitable for large-scale production and can easily cut through thick corrugated boards. On the other hand, electric sample cutters are powered by electricity and offer more flexibility in terms of portability and usage. They are commonly used for smaller production runs and can efficiently cut various types of corrugated materials.</p></p>
