<p><p>The Hemoditoxifier market includes two major types: Activated Carbon and Resin. Activated carbon is a highly porous material that effectively removes impurities and toxins from the bloodstream through a process called adsorption. It has a wide range of applications in water and air purification systems. On the other hand, resin is a type of polymer material that uses ion exchange to remove toxins from the blood. Resin-based hemoditoxifiers are often used in medical devices like dialysis machines to help filter harmful substances. Both types play a crucial role in detoxification processes.</p></p>
