<p><p>Centesis catheters are medical devices used for removing fluid or fluid samples from body cavities. The market for centesis catheters can be classified into two types: large bore and small bore. Large bore centesis catheters have a wider diameter, enabling faster fluid drainage and are commonly used in emergency or critical situations. On the other hand, small bore centesis catheters have a narrower diameter, allowing for slow or controlled fluid removal and are typically used for non-emergency or non-critical procedures. These different types cater to various medical needs and situations.</p></p>
