<p><strong>The Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gigabit Passive Optical Network (GPON) equipment market consists of three main types: Optical Line Terminal (OLT), Optical Network Terminal (ONT), and Passive Optical Splitters. OLT serves as the central communication hub, connecting the service provider's network to the subscriber's premises. ONT is installed at the subscriber's location, enabling the connection of multiple devices to the GPON network. Passive Optical Splitters are optical devices that divide the optical signal from the OLT into multiple signals and distribute them to different ONTs, allowing for efficient data transmission and sharing within the network.</p></p>
