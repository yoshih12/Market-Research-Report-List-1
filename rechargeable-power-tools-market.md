<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rechargeable Power Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nickle Cadmium (NiCd)</li><li>Nickle Metal Hydride (NiMH)</li><li>Lithium Ion (Li-Ion)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rechargeable power tools market can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. Nickle Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are the oldest and most commonly used type, offering a low cost but suffer from memory effect and toxicity concerns. Nickle Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries provide higher energy density and are more environmentally friendly than NiCd. Lithium Ion (Li-Ion) batteries offer the highest energy density, longer lifespan, and don't have memory effect, making them the most popular choice. Lastly, there are other emerging technologies in the market that aim to improve battery performance and address specific needs.</p></p>
