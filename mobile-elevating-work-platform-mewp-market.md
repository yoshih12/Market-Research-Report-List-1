<p><p>A Mobile Elevating Work Platform (MEWP) is a type of equipment designed to provide workers with a safe and efficient way to perform tasks at elevated heights. MEWPs are commonly used in construction, maintenance, and industrial applications.</p><p>The Mobile Elevating Work Platform (MEWP) Market has experienced significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. One of the key factors driving market growth is the increasing need for efficient and safe working conditions, especially in construction and infrastructure development projects. MEWPs offer enhanced safety features, such as fall protection systems and emergency lowering controls, which contribute to their rising popularity.</p><p>Technological advancements have also played a crucial role in the market growth of MEWPs. Manufacturers are increasingly developing innovative MEWPs with features such as telematics, self-propelled capabilities, and improved maneuverability.
