Additionally, the rising demand for electric vehicles (EVs) has also contributed to the market growth, as these vehicles require powerful starter motors to overcome the initial inertia.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in starter motor technologies are driving the market. Manufacturers are focusing on developing compact and lightweight Hi Torque starter motors, with improved efficiency and enhanced reliability.
