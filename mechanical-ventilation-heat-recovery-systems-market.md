<p><p>Mechanical Ventilation Heat Recovery Systems are divided into two main types: Whole House Heat Recovery and Single Room Heat Recovery. Whole House Heat Recovery systems are designed to provide heat recovery and ventilation for the entire house. They are installed centrally and use ductwork to distribute fresh air throughout the entire home, while simultaneously extracting stale air. Single Room Heat Recovery systems, on the other hand, are installed in individual rooms or smaller areas. They are typically used in spaces where ventilation and heat recovery are required on a smaller scale, such as offices or apartments.</p></p>
