<p><p>Schottky diodes are electronic components used in various applications. The market for Schottky diodes can be classified into two types based on the manufacturing technology used: Through Hole Technology and Surface Mount Technology. Through Hole Technology involves inserting the diode leads into holes on a printed circuit board and soldering them. Surface Mount Technology, on the other hand, mounts the diodes directly onto the surface of the board using solder paste and reflow soldering. Both technologies have their advantages and are utilized in different industries depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
