<p><strong>The VOC Gas Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC gas analyzer market includes two major types: PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. PID gas analyzers detect volatile organic compounds (VOCs) by ionizing them using ultraviolet light and measuring the resulting current. This type of gas analyzer is highly sensitive and can detect a wide range of VOCs. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor gas analyzers utilize a sensor composed of a heated metal oxide material that reacts with VOCs, causing a change in electrical conductivity which is measured to detect the presence and concentration of VOCs. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries for air quality monitoring and industrial safety applications.</p></p>
