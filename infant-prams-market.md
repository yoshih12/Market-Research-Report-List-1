<p><p>The infant prams market consists of two main types: travel systems and conventional type prams. Travel systems are versatile and include a detachable car seat that can be used in a vehicle and then attached to the stroller frame. This allows parents to easily transport their baby from the car to the stroller without disturbing them. On the other hand, conventional type prams are more traditional and designed specifically for strolling purposes. They do not have a detachable car seat option and are generally larger and more spacious for the baby.</p></p>
