<p><p>The Gynecological Treatment Table market consists of two main types: Electric Tables and Mechanical Tables. Mechanical Tables, on the other hand, rely on manual adjustment mechanisms, such as cranks or pneumatic systems, to change positions. Both types cater to the specific needs of gynecologists and aim to provide optimal comfort and accessibility for patients.</p></p>
