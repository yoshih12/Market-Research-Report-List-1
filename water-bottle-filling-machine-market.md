<p><p>The water bottle filling machine market can be categorized into three types: manual, automatic, and semi-automatic machines. Manual machines require human intervention for each step of the filling process, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Automatic machines perform all filling operations automatically, without any manual intervention, making them ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic machines combine the features of both manual and automatic machines, requiring some human involvement but also automating certain tasks. Each type of machine caters to different production capacities and operational requirements.</p></p>
