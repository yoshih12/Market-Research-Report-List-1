<p><ul><li>Analogue Input</li><li>Analogue Output</li><li>Analogue Input/Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PLC expansion modules refer to additional devices that can be connected to a programmable logic controller (PLC) to expand its functionality. Analogue input modules enable the PLC to receive signals from analog sensors or devices. Analogue output modules allow the PLC to send analog signals to control devices such as variable speed drives or valves. Analogue input/output modules combine both functionalities, enabling the PLC to receive and send analog signals. These expansion modules enhance the PLC's versatility and allow it to interface with a wider range of industrial processes or systems.</p></p>
