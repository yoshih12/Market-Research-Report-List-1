<p><p>Semiconductor etch equipment refers to the tools used in the manufacturing process of semiconductors to selectively remove materials for creating circuit patterns. There are two types of semiconductor etch equipment markets: dry etch equipment and wet etch equipment. Dry etch equipment uses plasma to remove materials, offering precise and controlled etching capabilities. Wet etch equipment, on the other hand, uses chemical solutions to selectively dissolve materials. Both types are crucial in the semiconductor industry for achieving desired circuit patterns and enhancing the performance of semiconductors.</p></p>
