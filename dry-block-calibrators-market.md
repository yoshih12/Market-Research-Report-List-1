<p><p>A dry block calibrator is a temperature calibration instrument used to calibrate temperature sensors and other temperature measuring devices accurately. It consists of a metal block with precision-drilled holes to hold the sensors and heating elements that provide stable and uniform temperatures. These calibrators are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, food processing, pharmaceuticals, and research laboratories.</p><p>The market for dry block calibrators has been growing steadily in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
