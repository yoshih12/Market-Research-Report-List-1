<p><ul><li>Grid-Connected Solar Microinverter</li><li>Grid-Off Solar Microinverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar microinverter market can be categorized into two types: grid-connected solar microinverters and grid-off solar microinverters. Grid-connected microinverters are designed to connect directly to the electrical grid, allowing the generated solar energy to be used in households or businesses. They provide efficient conversion of DC solar energy to AC power. On the other hand, grid-off microinverters are specifically used in off-grid systems where there is no access to utility grids. They store excess solar energy in batteries for later use and provide reliable power in remote areas or during power outages.</p></p>
