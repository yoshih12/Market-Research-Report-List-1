<p><p>Edible thickening agents, also known as food thickeners, are substances that increase the viscosity of a liquid without altering its other properties. These agents are commonly used in food and beverage industries to improve the texture, mouthfeel, and stability of products. Common types of edible thickening agents include starches (cornstarch, tapioca starch), gums (guar gum, xanthan gum), gelatins, and pectins.</p><p>The edible thickening agent market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing demand for convenience foods and processed foods, including soups, sauces, gravies, and desserts, is a major driving factor for the market. The rising geriatric population globally, which often suffers from dysphagia (swallowing difficulties), has also boosted the demand for food thickeners as they help in swallowing and preventing choking.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing awareness about various health benefits associated with edible thickening agents, such as improved digestion and reduced calorie intake, has propelled their usage. The growing vegan and vegetarian population has also favored the demand for plant-based edible thickening agents.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends. Manufacturers are focusing on developing natural and organic edible thickening agents to meet the increasing consumer preference for clean label and healthier food products. There is also a growing trend of using combinations of different thickeners to achieve unique textures and functionalities.</p><p>According to the provided information, the edible thickening agent market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period. Factors such as the rising demand for convenience foods, the increasing geriatric population, and the growing awareness about health benefits are likely to contribute to the market growth. Additionally, the development of natural and organic thickening agents and the emergence of innovative textures and functionalities through combinatorial approaches are expected to drive the market further.</p></p>
