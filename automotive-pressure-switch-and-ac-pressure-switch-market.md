<p><p>Automotive pressure switches are devices used in vehicles to monitor and control pressure levels in various systems. The dual function pressure switch is used for both high and low-pressure applications. High-pressure cut-out (HPCO) switches are designed to protect the compressor from damage due to excessively high pressures. Fan pressure switches control the operation of cooling fans. Compressor cycling switches regulate the on/off cycle of the compressor, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency. These various types of pressure switches play essential roles in maintaining the functioning of automotive systems.</p></p>
