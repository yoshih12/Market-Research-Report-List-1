<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Audio sensors are devices used to detect and measure sound waves in various applications. There are two types of audio sensor markets: analog output market and digital output market. </p><p>In the analog output market, audio sensors produce continuous electrical signals that are proportional to the sound waves detected. These signals can be further processed for various uses, such as amplification or analog-to-digital conversion.</p><p>On the other hand, the digital output market involves audio sensors that provide discrete digital signals, typically in the form of binary code, representing the sound waves detected. These signals are more easily processed by digital devices and can be used for tasks like digital signal processing or data storage.</p></p>
