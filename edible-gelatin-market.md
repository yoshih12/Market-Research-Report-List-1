<p><strong>The Edible Gelatin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alkali Preparation</li><li>Acid Preparation</li><li>Enzyme Preparation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The edible gelatin market is categorized into three types based on the preparation method: alkali preparation, acid preparation, and enzyme preparation. Alkali preparation involves the use of alkaline substances to break down collagen bonds. Acid preparation utilizes acids to dissolve collagen and extract gelatin. Enzyme preparation involves the use of enzymes to hydrolyze collagen. Each method offers unique characteristics and properties to the gelatin produced, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable method for their specific applications in the food and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
