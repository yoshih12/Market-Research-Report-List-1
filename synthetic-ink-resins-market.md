<p><p>Synthetic ink resins are categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and properties. Modified Rosin resins are derived from natural resins and have improved characteristics for ink applications. Hydrocarbon resins are petroleum-based and offer good adhesion and color properties. Acrylic resins provide excellent flexibility and durability. Polyamide resins are known for their chemical resistance and high temperature stability. Polyurethane resins are versatile and offer great abrasion resistance. Each type of resin caters to specific ink requirements, making them suitable for diverse applications.</p></p>
