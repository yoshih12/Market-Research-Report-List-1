WDM allows multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single fiber optic cable by dividing the available spectrum into different wavelengths. <p><ul><li>Wavelength Selection</li><li>Light Wave Amplification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wavelength Manager market encompasses two main types: Wavelength Selection and Light Wave Amplification. Wavelength Selection involves managing and manipulating the desired wavelengths of light signals to transmit information effectively in optical networks. This type of market focuses on devices and technologies that can selectively filter and route specific wavelengths without affecting others. On the other hand, Light Wave Amplification focuses on boosting the strength of light signals to enable longer transmission distances. This market involves technologies like erbium-doped fiber amplifiers that amplify the light without the need for conversion to electrical signals.</p></p>
