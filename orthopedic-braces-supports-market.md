<p><p>The Orthopedic Braces & Supports market is divided into two main types: Lower Extremity and Upper Extremity. Lower Extremity braces and supports are designed to assist and stabilize areas such as the knee, foot, ankle, and back. They provide support, reduce pain, and aid in the recovery process. On the other hand, Upper Extremity braces and supports focus on the shoulder, elbow, and wrist. These devices are used to immobilize these areas, relieve strain, and promote healing. Both types serve as crucial aids in managing and rehabilitating orthopedic injuries.</p></p>
