<p><p>ZigBee modules are devices used in wireless communications systems. The market for such modules can be segmented into three types based on frequency bands: 868MHz, 900MHz, and 2.4GHz. 868MHz ZigBee modules operate on a frequency band primarily used in Europe, offering good network coverage but with limited data transfer rates. 900MHz ZigBee modules provide a longer range than 868MHz modules and are commonly used in the Americas. 2.4GHz ZigBee modules offer faster data transfer rates and are widely used globally, although they have shorter range compared to the other two types.</p></p>
