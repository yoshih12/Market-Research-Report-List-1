<p><p>The touchless coffee machine market can be classified into two types: Connect Phone and Disconnect Phone. On the other hand, the Disconnect Phone market caters to users who prefer a simplified experience without phone connectivity. These coffee machines offer easy-to-use physical interfaces for brewing coffee without the need for smartphone integration.</p></p>
