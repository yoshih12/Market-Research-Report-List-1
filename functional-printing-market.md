<p><p>Functional printing refers to the use of printing technologies for the fabrication of functional electronic devices and components. The market types in functional printing include screen printing, gravure printing, flexography, inkjet, and others. Screen printing involves pushing ink through a mesh screen onto a substrate, while gravure printing uses an engraved cylinder to transfer ink onto the surface. Flexography is a rotary printing method often used for packaging materials. Inkjet printing involves spraying ink onto a substrate, and "others" refer to alternative printing methods such as aerosol jet printing and offset lithography.</p></p>
