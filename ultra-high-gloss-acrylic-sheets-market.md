<p><p>The Ultra High Gloss Acrylic Sheets market comprises two main types: Extruded Acrylic Sheet and Cast Acrylic Sheet. Extruded Acrylic Sheet is manufactured by continuously melting and extruding thermoplastic material, resulting in a more affordable option with lower light transmission and dimensional stability. On the other hand, Cast Acrylic Sheet is produced through a casting process using molds, making it more expensive but offering enhanced optical clarity and better resistance to external factors like UV radiation and chemicals. Both types cater to different market demands based on their specific characteristics and applications.</p></p>
