<p><p>Flat Panel Detectors (FPDs) are advanced imaging devices used in various industries such as healthcare and security. There are two main types of FPDs: Indirect FPDs and Direct FPDs. Indirect FPDs use a scintillator material to convert X-rays into visible light, which is then detected by an array of photodiodes. On the other hand, Direct FPDs directly convert X-rays into electrical signals using a semiconductor material. Each type of FPD offers unique advantages in terms of image quality, speed, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
