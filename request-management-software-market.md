<p><p>Request Management software is a tool that streamlines the process of managing and fulfilling requests within an organization. There are two main market types for this software: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based request management software is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy accessibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs. On the other hand, On-Premise request management software is installed locally on servers within the organization's infrastructure, providing more control and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Both market types aim to enhance request management efficiency, but with different deployment options.</p></p>
