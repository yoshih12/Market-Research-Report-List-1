<p><p>A static road roller, also known as a vibratory roller or roller compactor, is a heavy construction machinery used for compacting surfaces such as roads, highways, and parking lots. The static road roller is designed to improve the load-bearing capacity, smoothness, and durability of the road surface.</p><p>The global static road roller market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
